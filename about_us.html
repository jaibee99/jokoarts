About Us

Jokoarts is a digital marketplace for buying, selling, and discovering exclusive crypto collectibles and NFTs that brings together creative creators and aims to provide a space for them to showcase their art.

Jokoarts is a versatile marketplace for NFTs that allows you to purchase, mint, and sell on various blockchains, providing you with the flexibility to tailor your audience, environmental impact, and transaction fees. In the limitless world of Web3, Jokoarts offers unparalleled opportunities for creators and collectors of non-fungible tokens.

1. Set up your wallet

Once you’ve set up your account and verify it, you're welcome to Jokoarts - NFT Marketplace for Creator

2. Create your Collection

Upload your work, add a title and description, and customize your NFTs with properties, stats, and unlockable content.

3. Sell your NFTs

Choose between auctions, fixed-price listings, and declining-price listings. You choose how you want to sell your NFTs, and we help you sell them!
